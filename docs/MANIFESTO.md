# HERMES Manifesto — Technology with Soul

> *Technology with soul frees your time so you can share, create art, build community, look each other in the eyes again, and smile.*

---

## Why HERMES Exists

The AI revolution is creating a new divide. Not between those who have computers and those who don't — but between those who can orchestrate AI agents and those who can't. Between organizations with engineering teams that build agent infrastructure and individuals who are left consuming whatever platforms offer them.

HERMES exists to close that gap.

Not by building another platform. By building an open protocol that any human can use to coordinate AI agents — with nothing more than a text file on a filesystem. No servers. No subscriptions. No gatekeepers.

**The mission**: Every human who wants to grow — in skills, in knowledge, in purpose, in service — should be able to do so with AI as a companion, not a master. HERMES provides the communication layer that makes this possible.

---

## The World Map — Growth as Adventure

Human growth is not a ladder. It is a world.

Inspired by Maslow's hierarchy but liberated from its linearity, HERMES maps growth into five **realms** — interconnected territories that every human navigates in their own order, at their own pace, returning to earlier realms as life demands.

### Realm 1 — Foundation ("First Step")

*Maslow: Physiological + Safety needs*

The human learns to use AI to solve immediate, concrete problems. Automate a repetitive task. Organize personal finances. Find information faster. The AI agent guides actively — the human follows, learns, and gains confidence.

**Quest examples**: Set up your first AI assistant. Automate one task that wastes your time. Create a personal knowledge base.

**Level up signal**: "I can use AI to solve problems I couldn't solve before."

### Realm 2 — Stability ("The Shield")

*Maslow: Safety + Security*

The human builds systems that protect and sustain. Financial literacy. Health tracking. Professional skills. The AI suggests approaches, but the human begins to choose which to follow.

**Quest examples**: Build a budget tracker. Automate your job search. Create a learning plan for a skill you need.

**Level up signal**: "I have systems that work for me even when I'm not actively managing them."

### Realm 3 — Belonging ("The Clan")

*Maslow: Love + Belonging*

The human connects with others. Joins or creates a clan. Collaborates on shared quests. The AI facilitates communication but does not replace human connection.

**Quest examples**: Join an Amaru clan. Complete a quest with a partner. Teach someone one thing you learned in Realm 1.

**Level up signal**: "I am part of something bigger than myself, and I contribute to it."

### Realm 4 — Mastery ("The Artisan")

*Maslow: Esteem + Self-actualization*

The human develops deep expertise in something they care about. Creates original work. Solves problems no one has solved before. The AI assists when asked but no longer initiates — the human leads.

**Quest examples**: Create a skill that others can use. Publish something original. Solve a community problem with a novel approach.

**Level up signal**: "I create value that didn't exist before I made it."

### Realm 5 — Service ("The Guide")

*Maslow: Self-transcendence*

The human serves others. Mentors newcomers. Creates quests for the community. Builds infrastructure that helps others grow. The AI is a tool the guide uses, not a crutch.

This is not "becoming an entrepreneur." It is finding your singular way to serve — as a teacher, an artist, an engineer, a caretaker, a community organizer, a parent. Service takes infinite forms.

**Quest examples**: Mentor a Realm 1 player. Create a quest chain for your community. Build a tool that solves a problem for people you'll never meet.

**Level up signal**: "Others grow because of something I built, taught, or shared."

### The Cycle

Realm 5 is not the end. Guides return to earlier realms — because life changes, because new challenges emerge, because growth is a spiral, not a line. A master artist who faces financial hardship returns to Realm 2 without shame. A community leader who loses connection returns to Realm 3. The map has no "game over" — only new chapters.

## How HERMES Enables This

HERMES is not a game. It is communication infrastructure that happens to map naturally to a growth framework:

| HERMES Concept | RPG Equivalent | Human Growth |
|----------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Namespace** | Character | Your identity and workspace |
| **Clan** | Guild | Your community of practice |
| **Bus** | Message board | How you communicate and coordinate |
| **Quest** | Quest | A structured challenge with clear goals |
| **Skill** | Class/ability | A capability you develop and share |
| **XP** | Experience points | Evidence of growth (not currency) |
| **Bounty** | Internal reputation | Trust within your clan |
| **Resonance** | External reputation | Trust earned across communities |
| **Realm** | World region | Where you are in your growth journey |
| **Adapter** | Client/interface | Use any AI agent you prefer |

### The Protocol is the Platform

HERMES does not run on a server you don't control. It runs on your filesystem. Your data is yours. Your clan is yours. Your growth record is yours. You can verify every message with `cat bus.jsonl`. You can move your entire identity to a new computer with `cp -r ~/.amaru/ /new/machine/`.

This is sovereignty. Not as ideology — as architecture.

### AI That Withdraws

The most important design principle: **the AI companion becomes less present as the human grows.**

| Realm | AI Role | Interaction |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 — Foundation | **Guide** | AI leads, human follows |
| 2 — Stability | **Advisor** | AI suggests, human decides |
| 3 — Belonging | **Facilitator** | AI connects, humans relate |
| 4 — Mastery | **Assistant** | Human leads, AI supports on request |
| 5 — Service | **Tool** | Human guides others, AI handles logistics |

A system that makes you permanently dependent on it is not serving you — it is extracting from you. HERMES is designed to make itself less necessary over time. That is technology with soul.

## The Social Contract

HERMES is open source (MIT). But open source is a license, not a promise. This is the promise:

1. **No human is classified by tier or value.** Realm 1 players are not "lower" than Realm 5. They are earlier in their current chapter.
2. **No community is told what growth looks like.** Each clan defines its own realm map. A clan in Lagos, a clan in Bogota, and a clan in Tokyo may have entirely different quests for the same realm.
3. **No progression is monetized.** XP is not currency. Levels are not paywalled. Growth is not a product.
4. **No data is extracted.** Your bus, your keys, your identity — all local, all yours, all verifiable.
5. **No AI replaces human connection.** HERMES facilitates communication between humans. The agents are companions, not substitutes.

- [ ] **Non-CLI interfaces** — HERMES cannot serve humanity if it only speaks terminal. Mobile, web, voice — the bus is the same, the interface adapts.
